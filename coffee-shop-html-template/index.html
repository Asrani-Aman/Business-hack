About Us

BRINGING CUTURE AGAIN

Our Story

WE as youth , realise that our Indian culture is supressing day by day and handicrafts and in this way our Indian culture will extinct. So as a native we have decided to bring back our Indian culture India has a heritage of handloom and weaving that is unique and the largest anywhere in the world. However, traditional arts like handicrafts, weaving, dying, etc have been undergoing a process of slow death pertaining to a multitude of factors. The current market ecosystem for artisans, namely weavers and handloom workers, actively exploits the manufacturers. The retailers who buy manufactured goods in bulk, sell them at exorbitantly high prices. This, coupled with a lack of support and implementation of policies is slowly killing the handloom industry.
